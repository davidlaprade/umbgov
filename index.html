Student Senate Special Elections Wednesday, September 3, 2014

The Student Senate has vacancies for the 2014-2015 academic year. If you are interested in running, then please go here.

Students must be matriculated, undergraduates in good academic and disciplinary standing and have a minimum 2.5 cumulative GPA.

Deadline to submit nominations is Friday, September 19th at midnight and elections will be Wednesday, September 24 at 3 pm in the CC Alumni Lounge (2nd floor). Students must have Wednesdays from 3-4 pm open to participate in Student Senate.

Posted by Shelby Harris
Welcome Back! Friday, August 29th, 2014

The Undergraduate Student Government welcomes you back for another great academic year!

Returning clubs have until Friday, September 19 to complete training and reactivate.

New clubs applications are due by September 19 for fall activation. Go to https://umb.collegiatelink.net/ for more information.

Special Elections for vacant student senate seats will be Wednesday, September 24th at 3 pm in the Alumni Lounge. Online nomination forms will be available by Wednesday, September 3.

Posted by Shelby Harris
